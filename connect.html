📋 How It Works

  1. Your Instagram Business account is connected via Meta Graph API
  2. When someone DMs or comments a keyword, Meta sends a webhook to PinGuru
  3. PinGuru matches the trigger to your automation rules
  4. An automated reply is sent back within seconds
⚠️ Note: During development mode, automated replies only work for Instagram Tester accounts. Full access requires Meta App Review approval.
